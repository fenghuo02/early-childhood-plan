## 主题词汇表（按季度追加）

hello, bye, yes, no, please, thank you, head, eye, nose, mouth, ear, hand, foot, arm, leg, jump, sit, stand, clap, stop

red, blue, yellow, green, black, white, one, two, three, four, five, six, seven, eight, nine, ten, big, small, what, color

### Q3 追加 25 词

cat, dog, bird, fish, duck, cow, pig, apple, banana, orange, milk, water, bread, egg, eat, drink, sleep, run, walk, like, don't, moo, woof, meow, yum

### Q4 追加 25 词

mom, dad, baby, sun, rain, cloud, hot, cold, happy, sad, angry, tired, home, bed, book, toy, share, wait, help, me, you, this, that, see, look

## TPR 指令库（随时用）

```
Stand up. / Sit down. / Jump. / Stop.
Touch your head. / Show me red. / Give me five.
Open the book. / Close the book.
Walk to the door. / Come here.
```

一次给 **1 个指令**；熟练后连 2 个。
